Frequently Asked Questions

How long does the consultation process take?

Timelines vary by package. Starter packages typically deliver within 7 business days, Professional within 14 days, and Enterprise projects span 2-3 months depending on scope.

Are consultations conducted in-person or online?

We offer both options. Initial consultations are typically conducted via video call. Site assessments for Professional and Enterprise packages include in-person visits (Australia-wide).

Can I combine templates with consultation services?

Absolutely! Many clients purchase templates as a foundation and add consultation services to customize them. We offer discounts for combined purchases.

What if I need changes after the project is complete?

All packages include a defined support period. After that, we offer affordable hourly rates for updates or you can upgrade to ongoing support packages.
